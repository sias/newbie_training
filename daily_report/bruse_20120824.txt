			Use Money  To Encourage  The Children To Study 
	We know the children's learning achievement always troubled the parents.The  children  like to play  than 
to study,they always take study as a boring thing.But it is important to give the children  good study environment.
	As the parents how do you encourage your children to study?Some parents are strict with the children ,they 
let their children to study in that method.Some parents stay beside  the children to supervise them.Now there are 
some parents use money to encourage their children to study,that may make great influence on the children.They may 
study hard in order to achieve the money,then they can  buy something they want to own.In this way the parent  don't 
need spend much time to worry about the children's study. But,some one don't agree with the method,they think we should 
not  use money to  influence children ,that may lead them to get bad habit of wasting money. 
	I don't agree with the parents to use money too,I hope they can use a better way that is benefit to the children's growth.
