				On the way to company							
	It is time for us to work from Monday to Friday,we get up early and 
go quickly on the road.Are you thinking to reach the company as soon as
possible only.It is the pressure  of work make us forget to find the sights
around us.In fact there are many interesting sights on our way to work.
	The air in the morning is a particular gift what the god give people,
we can breath the fresh air,at the same time,do you notice the moving advertisements
on the bus,many buses run past us and the advertisement looks like a cartoon movie.
From the movie we can not only know about some informations but also enjoy a  movie.
Every day I went to the company by bus.I am interested in listening the morning radio
broadcast on the bus,I know the humor host named breakfast,he talk jokes every morning
and broad music.It's nice to listen to broadcast when you sit on the soft seat.
