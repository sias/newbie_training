				Crabs
	The crab in YangCheng lake is famous on the world,they have another  
name called golden leg crabs.The crabs have many good points,they are fat with 
a green shell and white belly.Although they come from the mud but there are no 
mud on their body,besides the crabs are delicious and nutrient-rich.So the price 
is high,the report says that Yangcheng lake has produce two tons crabs in 2012.
	Autumn is the perfect time to eat the crabs near YangCheng lake,every 
autumn many people come here in order to eat the crabs.If you go to YangCheng 
lake for the crabs you can get another surprise,the beautiful scenery will 
make you fall in love with SuZhou.
