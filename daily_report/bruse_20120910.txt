				Teacher's  Day
	It is Teacher's Day today,teachers are the greatest people in the world.
In the past twenty years,I have lived under many teachers' protects.The teacher not 
only teach us the knowledge but also teach us how to be a real people.In class 
they are very strict to us,in life they are our friends.When we have  trouble they
always come to give us a hand without want anything.We always take the teacher as
our parents,they teach us so many knowledge and take the most important part in our
life.I appreciate every teacher,thanks for your selfless devotion.
	Thanks to you, my dear teacher. On the voyage of life, you have rised
the light of hope for me. What you have done enriches my mind and broadens my view. On
this day I honour you sincerely.
